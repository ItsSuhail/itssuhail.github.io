Simple Cubic (SC)

Lattice
1 Rank (Z) ?
52.4% Packing Eff.
6 Coord. No.
Edge-Radius Relation: a = 2r

In a simple cubic lattice, atoms occupy only the corners of the cube. They touch along the edges, giving the coordination number of 6.

NCERT Numerical Helper: Density Calculator

\(d = \frac{Z \times M}{N_A \times a^3}\)

Calculated Density: 2.16 g/cm³

Revision Notes

  • Atoms at corners contribute 1/8 to the unit cell.
  • Simple cubic has 8 corner atoms, so \(8 \times 1/8 = 1\) atom per cell.
  • Octahedral voids are 4 times Z, Tetrahedral voids are 2 times Z (for FCC).
